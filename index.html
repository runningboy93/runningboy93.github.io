2016 Presidential Campaign

April's primaries will soon give way to nominees from the (at least) two most significant American political parties as they move from battling fellow party members in an attempt to secure their party's nomination, to competing against each other to determine who will become the 45th President of the United States on November 8, 2016.

Primary Season


Remember these GH Ads!
Primaries in each party began in February, 2016 with the Iowa Caucus on February 1 and the New Hampshire Primary on February 9. The GOP concludes their primaries on June 7th when five states award winner-take-all delegates, and the Democratic Party concludes their nominating round on June 14 in a District of Columbia Primary.
